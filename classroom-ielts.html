Academic vs General Training (Important)

  • Reading: Academic uses longer, research-style texts; General Training uses workplace/everyday materials with simpler text flow.
  • Writing Task 1: Academic requires data/chart/process summary; General Training requires letter writing for practical situations.
  • Writing Task 2: Both versions require an essay, but prompt tone and context can differ.
