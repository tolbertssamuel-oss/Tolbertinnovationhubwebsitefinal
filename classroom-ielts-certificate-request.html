IELTS Certificate Request

Request Your IELTS Completion Certificate

Students who successfully complete the IELTS Mock Examination can request their Certificate of Completion from Tolbert Innovation Hub.

To process the certificate, a certificate preparation fee of $5 USD is required.

Please complete the form below so we can verify your mock exam results and prepare your certificate.

Certificates can be:

  • Sent to you by email
  • Collected in person at the Tolbert Innovation Hub office

Processing time: 24–48 hours after payment and verification.
