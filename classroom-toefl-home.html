TOEFL Scoring & Strategy (Separate from IELTS)

  • Scoring: 0–120 total score (Reading, Listening, Speaking, Writing).
  • Writing Strategy: Integrated + Independent writing response planning.
  • Speaking Strategy: Computer-based 4 timed speaking tasks.

TOEFL iBT Structure (Important)

  • Reading: Academic passages with evidence-based questions and reasoning.
  • Listening: Lecture and conversation comprehension with strong note-taking.
  • Speaking: Four timed tasks with clear organization and fluency.
  • Writing: Integrated and independent writing with coherence and academic vocabulary.
