TOEFL iBT Structure (Important)

  • Reading: Academic passages with evidence-based questions that test understanding and reasoning.
  • Listening: Conversations and lectures that require strong note-taking and idea tracking.
  • Speaking: Independent and integrated tasks with clear structure under strict timing.
  • Writing: Integrated and independent responses that require clarity, coherence, and academic language.
